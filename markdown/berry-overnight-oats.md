# Berry Overnight Oats

## Ingredients
* 1/2 cup rolled oats
* 1/4 cup chia seeds
* 1 cup almond milk
* 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 2 tablespoons blueberries (fresh)
* 2 tablespoons raspberries (fresh)
* 2 teaspoons honey
* 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
* 2 tablespoons almonds (shaved)

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients and mix well. Pour into an airtight container, cover, and refrigerate overnight. Serve cold with additional berries and almonds if desired.
